Ministry Objectives & Values

Our Ministry Objectives

Guided by our faith, we strive to achieve these key objectives in our community and beyond

Spiritual Growth

To nurture the spiritual development of our members through biblical teaching, worship, and prayer.

Community Building

To create a welcoming, supportive community where all can find belonging and fellowship.

Service & Outreach

To serve our local and global community through practical help and sharing the Gospel.

Discipleship

To equip believers to grow in their faith and become mature followers of Christ.

Spreading faith, hope, and love to communities around the world since 2022
